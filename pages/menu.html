HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Antidote Rush!


Your objective is to survive waves of zombies until the antidote is ready.


1. Select your difficulty.

2. Use WASD to move.

3. Point and click to shoot.

4. Press 1, 2, 3 to switch weapons.
